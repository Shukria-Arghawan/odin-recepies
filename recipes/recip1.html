Okra


Okra is a healthy and delicious food that i like it

ingredients

  • Okra
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato & onion

How to cook Okra?

  • Wash the okra thoroughly and pat it dry with a paper towel.
  • Trim off the stems and cut the okra into desired sizes.
  • Heat a small amount of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the okra to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 10-15 minutes or until it becomes tender
  • Season with salt, pepper, and any other desired spices or herbs.
  • Serve the cooked okra as a side dish or incorporate it into your favorite recipes.
